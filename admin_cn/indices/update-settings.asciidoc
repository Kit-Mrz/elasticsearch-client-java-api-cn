[[java-admin-indices-update-settings]]
==== 更新索引设置

通过调用以下代码你可以更改索引设置:

[source,java]
--------------------------------------------------
client.admin().indices().prepareUpdateSettings("twitter")   <1>
        .setSettings(Settings.builder()                     <2>
                .put("index.number_of_replicas", 0)
        )
        .get();
--------------------------------------------------
<1> 待更新的索引
<2> 设置
